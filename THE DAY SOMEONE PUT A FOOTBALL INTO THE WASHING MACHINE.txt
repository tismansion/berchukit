THE DAY SOMEONE PUT A FOOTBALL INTO THE WASHING MACHINE


Derek Great, a user from Redhair Centre, found a football lying dirty on the road.
A footballer called Lamb Chops had kicked it into the road.
when he was caught Lamb Chops got sent to Schoolstal for being cruel to a football.


The dirty football got sent to a launderette and put into the washing machine in order to clean it up.
Once this was done the football got sent to a football clinic where it would remain until it was well enough to  return to the football pitch.
