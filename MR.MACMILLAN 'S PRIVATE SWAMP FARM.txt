                       MR. MACMILLAN 'S  PRIVATE SWAMP FARM








Mr. Macmillan's  Private Swamp farm was a plot of land backing onto Ramsden Hall, owned by Mr. Macmillan, an Essex landowner who came from Billericay Regis. All his crops were grown in a giant swamp. He also had a share of the Doggeries, an animal farm in Ramsden Hall School.
Mr. Macmillan often went to Ramsden Doghouse to buy his groceries, which included blue butter, Gants Hill cheese, corn lamb, frog's milk, beef marmalade,
broccoletuce, the lot.
